Cartoon Lowpoly Forest Animals

 

Requires Unity 2018.2.3f1 or higher.



Cartoon Lowpoly Forest Animals pack contains 9 stylised animated models of adorable forest animals.


The pack includes a prefab of three wise bears, a lonely owl, a proud hawk, a family of hedgehogs and a surprised piglet and 4 different trees.
the models have walk, jump, fly and idle animations.

the models have unique hand painted textures with a normal map and a roughness map with the sizes of  1024x1024.

They have good model topology with a polycount between 200-1000 polygons. 

You have animation scripts For all the models and animation controllers. If you press 1, 2, 3, 4 you can preview the animations.
